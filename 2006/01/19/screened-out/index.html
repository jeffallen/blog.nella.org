Screened out

January 19, 2006 · 1 min · jra

I got a letter from MSF today informing me that they have reviewed my application and I won’t be getting an interview, and won’t be working for them any time soon.

I’m disappointed, but not deterred. This simply means I can’t get off easy and fill in only one application, as I was hoping to do. I now have a huge amount of work in front of me as I research other organizations and chose some to apply to.

So, before I think too much more about the “disappointed” part, I’m off to work on the “undeterred” part. I’ve got a lot of work to do, and my new self-imposed deadline is to have five applications in process by next Friday. Wish me luck!
